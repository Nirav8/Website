Our staff goes through intense and comprehensive training. They are knowledgeable in all areas of our business. Most importantly they are trained to provide extremely high quality service that focuses on meeting the needs of our customers.

It all began with two friends in business, a sweet tooth and a dream. In 2007, B 4 Bakery was founded with the goal of providing our customers with classic Indian baked goods in a warm & friendly atmosphere. Having 11 years experience of Restaurant Industry and a goal to serve the best, we started “Atul Bakery” on 26th January, 2007. Today we are respected as a leading brand in the bakery industry.

The moment you step into the bakery... the scent of Cakes, Pastries, Biscuits and many more Bakery Products catches your attention.
